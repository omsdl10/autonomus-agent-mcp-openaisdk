# Banoffee Pie Recipe

An easy family favourite with buttery pastry and sweet dulce de leche. Served best with a generous dollop of cream.

## Ingredients
- 4 bananas, sliced
- 394g caramel or dulce de leche
- 300ml double cream
- Dark chocolate (optional, for garnish)

### For the pastry:
- 100g butter, chilled (plus extra for greasing)
- 200g plain flour
- 1 medium egg, separated
- 1 tbsp golden caster sugar

## Method
1. Make the pastry case: In a food processor, pulse butter and flour until it resembles fresh breadcrumbs. Add egg yolk and sugar, pulse again. Add very cold water one tablespoon at a time until dough comes together.
2. Knead dough gently by hand to form a smooth ball, wrap in cling film, and chill for 30 minutes.
3. Heat oven to 190C/170C fan/gas 4. Grease a 23cm loose bottomed fluted tart tin.
4. Roll out chilled pastry on floured surface, line the tin, leaving about 0.5 cm proud. Trim edges.
5. Line base with baking parchment, weigh down with baking beans or uncooked rice. Bake for 15 mins.
6. Remove paper and beans, brush pastry surface with beaten egg white. Bake for another 15-20 mins until crisp and golden. Cool completely.
7. Spread half the caramel on the pastry case, layer sliced bananas, cover with remaining caramel. Chill.
8. Whip cream until thick and billowy, spoon over the tart.
9. To serve, optionally grate dark chocolate over the top.

## Serving
- Serves 8 to 10
- Keep chilled until serving

Enjoy your delicious Banoffee Pie!
